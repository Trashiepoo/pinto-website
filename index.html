ABOUT US

During the 2020 Pandemic Pinto Muay Thai was started in a garage. Pinto Muay Thai is the traditional style Muay Thai Gym in the Henderson area. All coaches at Pinto Muay Thai are knowledgeable in the art and have many years of coaching and fight experience.


PMT hosts Muay Thai classes for all experience levels, Jr Muay Thai classes (7-12years old), cardio Muay Thai and Pro level Muay Thai.


For inquiries, please email pintomuaythai@gmail.com


Head Coach Danny Pinto is a Professional Muay Thai fighter here in the Las Vegas area. He has been training and fighting for over 16 years and has trained and fought in Thailand several times. Coach Danny made his professional debut in Thailand and is the USMTA Nevada 140lb Champion. He is currently an active fighter which makes him a student of the game just like all of you.


Coach Sean is well respected in the Muay Thai community and has over two decades of experience coaching champions from all over the world- Coach Danny being one of them. Coach Sean's style is heavy boxing, quick counters and fast paced. Creating the perfect balance to Coach Danny's more traditional style.


Coach Suzy has been a student of Muay Thai for over 15 years and has several fights as an amateur. She is Coach Dannys competition pad holder and a certified nutritionist.


Our passion for the sport and love of teaching Muay Thai truly make Pinto Muay Thai a special place.
